👨‍💻 Personal Information

My name is Kevin Zhang and I would be pursuing my master's degree in theoretical and experimental linguistics starting from Fall 2020. I am curious about an array of phenomena that are related to languages and the mysterious human language faculty. Most of my current research lies in the production, perception, and processing of speech sounds in L1 and L2 learners' minds. Besides, I am also excited to learn about all the subdiscinplines of linguistics as I am only an entry-level linguistics major. Feel free to reach me if you share similar interests!

⛹ My life

Apart from academic studies, I often serve as an interpreter and participated in several events and part-time jobs. I am also a self-employed online language tutor on bilibili.com, providing affordable English and Spanish courses for Chinese nitizens. My channel's name is Enrique多语言. Besides, I am also an enthusiat in Japanese language and culture. In my spare time, I often sing Enka, a special kind of music popular in the second half of the 20th century. I deem Enka an indispensable part of my life!
